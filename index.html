How to Play Hextris

Game Controls

Use your keyboard arrows or swipe motions on mobile to move and rotate the falling hexagonal blocks. Place them strategically to match colors.

Matching Mechanics

When three sides of the same color connect, those sides will disappear, making room for more blocks. The longer you play, the faster blocks will fall!

Scoring System

Earn points for each cleared side. Chain reactions and combos will boost your score. How high can you go? Challenge your friends to beat your high score!

About Hextris

Hextris is an innovative puzzle game that combines elements of classic block-falling games with a hexagonal twist. Instead of traditional square blocks, Hextris challenges players with hexagonal shapes that must be matched based on color.

The game was designed to provide a fresh take on the puzzle genre, offering both casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts a new challenge that tests spatial reasoning and strategic thinking.

With its minimalist design and addictive gameplay, Hextris creates the perfect balance between accessibility and challenge. The rules are simple to understand, but mastering the game requires practice and skill.

"Hextris takes the classic block-matching concept and transforms it into something fresh and engaging with its hexagonal approach."

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session during a break or a challenging puzzle to test your skills, Hextris offers an engaging experience that's easy to learn but difficult to master.
